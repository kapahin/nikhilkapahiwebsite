About "The Inner Scorecard"

The Inner Scorecard is a podcast dedicated to exploring success from the inside out. Whether you're building a business, navigating new parenthood, or refining your mindset, this is a space for real, honest conversations about growth and fulfillment.

What to Expect

  • Business & Finance: Practical insights on launching and scaling ventures, smart investing, and pursuing financial independence.
  • Mental Models & Mindset: Deep dives into routines, habits, and mindsets that empower people to consistently level up.
  • Science & Tech: Clear and engaging explorations of AI, machine learning, and emerging technologies.
  • Community & Impact: Inspiring stories of individuals creating meaningful change—big and small.

Meet Your Host

I'm Nikhil Kapahi—a scientist, investor, entrepreneur, and new dad with a passion for mindfulness and personal growth. I believe success is best measured by your own internal standards, and through this podcast, I'm sharing the frameworks, failures, and reflections that shape how we define our inner scorecards.
